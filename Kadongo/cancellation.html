1. Policy Overview

Kadongo Resort understands that travel plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent, balancing flexibility for guests with operational needs for our remote island location.

All cancellation terms are confirmed at time of booking and detailed in your reservation confirmation email.

3. Reservation Modifications

  • Date Changes: Requested changes to arrival/departure dates are subject to availability and may incur rate differences. Changes made within 14 days of arrival may be treated as a cancellation + new booking.
  • Room Type Changes: Upgrades subject to availability and price difference. Downgrades may incur cancellation fees on the original booking.
  • Guest Count Changes: Adding guests may require rate adjustment. Reducing guest count follows standard cancellation timeline for the removed portion.

All modifications must be requested in writing via email to reservations@kadongoresort.com.

4. No-Show Policy

If you do not arrive on your scheduled check-in date and have not contacted us to cancel or modify:

  • The full reservation amount will be charged
  • No refund will be issued
  • Future bookings may require full prepayment

We recommend notifying us of delays due to travel disruptions (flights, weather) as soon as possible—we may offer flexibility on a case-by-case basis.

5. Exceptions & Force Majeure

We may waive cancellation fees in exceptional circumstances, including:

  • Government travel restrictions or border closures
  • Documented medical emergencies (with physician note)
  • Natural disasters affecting travel to Mfangano Island
  • Resort closure due to unforeseen circumstances

Requests for exception must be submitted with supporting documentation to guestrelations@kadongoresort.com for review.

6. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 7–14 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Bank processing times may add 3–5 additional business days
  • You will receive email confirmation once the refund is initiated
