The Experience

Convenience Meets Serenity

Located on the pristine shores of Mfangano Island, just meters from the ferry terminal. Kadongo Resort offers an intimate sanctuary where numerous trees create a calm natural environment filled with fresh lake breezes, dappled shade, and profound tranquility.

Our strategic position near local transport allows guests to explore the island's rich cultural heritage while returning to a haven of refined comfort.

Heritage

Discover Mfangano's Soul

Immerse yourself in the rich history of the Abasuba people. Our curated cultural experiences connect you with traditions that have thrived for thousands of years.

Mawanga Rock Art

Ancient paintings believed to be 1,000-4,000 years old. A site of deep spiritual significance historically associated with rainmaking rituals.

Kwitone Shelter

Hike through scenic forest trails to discover geometric paintings reflecting early human creativity and cultural traditions on the island.

Abasuba Museum

A cultural center preserving heritage with guided tours to historic rock art sites and insights into local Luo traditions.
